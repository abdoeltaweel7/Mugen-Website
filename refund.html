Refund Policy

Last updated: January 30, 2026

At Mugen AI, we strive to deliver high-quality services that meet your expectations. Due to the nature of digital services, please review our refund policy carefully before making a purchase.

Key Points

  • Setup fees and completed work are non-refundable
  • Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of purchase
  • Approved refunds processed within 7-14 business days

1 Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Setup fees and onboarding costs
  • Services that have been delivered or completed
  • Leads that have already been generated and delivered
  • Custom development work that has commenced
  • Consultation time that has been used
  • Third-party costs incurred on your behalf

2 Refund Eligibility

You may be eligible for a refund if:

  • Service has not yet started or been delivered
  • There was a billing error or duplicate charge
  • We are unable to deliver the agreed-upon service
  • Request is made within 7 days of purchase

Each refund request is reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

3 How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Email us at support@mugenai.site with subject: "Refund Request"
  2. Include your order/invoice number
  3. Provide a detailed reason for the refund request
  4. Include any relevant documentation or screenshots

We will respond to your request within 2-3 business days.

4 Refund Processing

If your refund is approved:

  • Refunds will be processed to the original payment method
  • Processing time: 7-14 business days
  • Bank processing may add additional days
  • You will receive email confirmation when processed

5 Subscription Cancellations

For subscription-based services:

  • You may cancel your subscription at any time
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period
  • No partial refunds for unused portions of the billing period
  • You retain access until the end of your paid period

To cancel, email us at least 5 business days before your next billing date.

6 Service Credits

In some cases, instead of a refund, we may offer:

  • Service credits for future use
  • Extended service period
  • Complimentary add-ons or upgrades

Service credits are valid for 12 months from the date of issue.

7 Disputes

If you disagree with our refund decision:

  • You may request a review by emailing support@mugenai.site
  • Include any additional information supporting your case
  • We will conduct a final review within 5 business days

8 Chargebacks

We encourage you to contact us directly before initiating a chargeback. Chargebacks initiated without prior communication may result in:

  • Immediate suspension of services
  • Account termination
  • Collection of outstanding amounts
